Accommodation

Guests have access to the deck, screened gazebo by the river, and trails across the ranch. The riverfront is a great area to enjoy a BBQ or campfire, play games, or just enjoy the serenity of this stretch of the Colorado River. Guests can also explore the 45-acre riparian woodland, which is a great place to see an array of wildlife. The ranch also has an indoor storage area for guests’ bikes, kayaks, paddle boards and other toys.*

The Guest House


Previous Next

Sleeping up to 8 guests, the Guest House offers fresh and comfortable accommodation which can be enjoyed all year round. The sleeping options are flexible and there are two bathrooms. The open-plan living area is equally well-suited for entertaining or family gatherings. There is also a patio area with seating, a fire pit to warm up on those cool evenings, a BBQ, and fantastic views!

The Loft is a studio apartment which sleeps up to 4 guests. It has a private wrap-around deck overlooking the property and great views of Mt. Garfield. The open-plan space is perfect for socializing or quiet relaxation after a busy day enjoying the outdoors.
